A brief history of the Bazaar

Back in 2017, before my mandatory youth service corp AKA serving the fatherland, I was at home for 6 months. I was bored and depressed so naturally I sought an escape.

Fashion has always been my thing and so I thought to myself, why not make other people look good and feel good as well.

That was how the Bazaar was born.
